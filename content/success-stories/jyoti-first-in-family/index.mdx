Jyoti is one of three sisters. She's in grade ten now at Khagrachari Model — a milestone no one in her extended family has reached before.

Her sponsorship covers tuition, the science-track lab fees that used to be the household's quiet deal-breaker, and a monthly stipend that offsets the domestic labor she would otherwise be doing. Her younger sisters are in grades four and six, both enrolled under the Girls' Scholarship Fund.
