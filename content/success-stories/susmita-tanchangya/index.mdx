পাহাড়ের কোলে বেড়ে ওঠা এক ফুল সুস্মিতা তঞ্চঙ্গ্যা। সবুজ মায়ায় নিজেকে জড়িয়ে উচ্ছল, প্রানবন্ত সুস্মিতা আজ এইচএসসিতে জিপিএ ৫ প্রাপ্ত শিক্ষার্থী।

Susmita Tanchangya comes from Noajhiri village, Rajsthuali Thana, in Chattogram District. Her father works in agriculture; her mother weaves fabric to add to the household income. In a region where many girls leave school after primary, Susmita kept going.

She earned a **GPA of 4.89 in her SSC** exams at Rajsthuali Toitong Para Government High School, then went on to score **GPA 5.00 in her HSC Science** exams — a milestone for any student, and rare in her village. She tutored younger students through high school to support herself.

Susmita joined Bridging Generations as a ninth-grade scholarship recipient in 2021. She was mentored first by Bhikku Rebatadham, and later by Bridging Generations staff through twelfth grade. The scholarship covered tuition, exam fees, and the daily materials that would otherwise have ended her schooling.

She now aspires to become a doctor. Her story is the through-line Bridging Generations was founded to build — a child from a poor Hill Tracts village finishing higher secondary at the top of her cohort, with a clear path forward.
