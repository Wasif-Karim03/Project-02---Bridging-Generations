Mithun's family are subsistence farmers in a hill village two hours above Bandarban Sadar. Until grade four, he missed roughly a month of school each autumn — the harvest always came first.

The meal program arrived the year he started grade five. Autumn attendance stayed unbroken from that year on. Mithun graduated at the top of his grade-twelve cohort last week. He's applying to teacher training colleges.
