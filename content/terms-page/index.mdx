Thank you for visiting bridginggenerations.org. By using this site you agree to the terms below. We have kept the language plain; if anything is unclear, write to us and we will explain it in more detail.

## Who we are

Bridging Generations is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization registered in the United States. Our programs operate in the Chittagong Hill Tracts, Bangladesh, in partnership with local schools and community leaders. Our EIN and mailing address are listed on the [About page](/about).

## How donations are handled

- Donations are processed by **Givebutter**, a third-party payment platform. Your card information is handled by Givebutter and never touches our servers.
- Tax-deductible receipts are issued automatically by Givebutter at the time of your gift. U.S. tax-deduction rules apply only to U.S. taxpayers.
- Every project-designated gift is applied to the program you selected. Unrestricted gifts support the general operating fund, which covers tuition, meals, materials, and the small set of overhead costs required to run the organization.
- Recurring gifts can be modified or cancelled at any time through the link in your Givebutter receipt.

## Privacy

- The contact form uses **Resend** to deliver your message to the board inbox. We use your email only to reply to you. We do not add you to any marketing list, we do not share it with third parties, and we do not run automated outreach on incoming messages.
- Donor records are held by Givebutter under their [privacy policy](https://givebutter.com/privacy). We view donor information only to acknowledge gifts and prepare annual reports.
- This site uses lightweight, cookie-free analytics to measure page views. No personal information is collected.

## Photography and student likenesses

- Student portraits appear on this site only where a signed release is on file.
- If you are a student, parent, or guardian and you wish to revoke a photo release, contact us. The photo will be removed within a few business days on the next deploy.

## Changes to these terms

We may update these terms occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Material changes will be announced on the home page for a week following the update.
