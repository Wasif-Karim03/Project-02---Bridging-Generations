## Overview

Bridging Generations' core program is direct scholarship sponsorship: pairing a single donor (or a small group of donors) with a single student for the duration of one school year. The sponsorship covers the costs that, in our experience, are the most frequent reason families pull a student out of school.

Each sponsorship is renewed term by term, contingent on the student keeping the eligibility terms. We never collect more than the actual amount needed to fund a student's term.

## Eligibility

Anyone may apply. The board approves applications against four criteria:

- The applicant attends one of our partner schools (or one we are evaluating).
- Family income falls below the regional threshold the board reviews each cycle.
- A parent or legal guardian provides written consent.
- The applicant is currently enrolled.

## What sponsorship covers

- **Tuition** for the term.
- **Books**, **uniform**, and **exam fees**.
- **Daily lunch** at schools with a meal program.
- **Materials**: notebooks, pens, calculators where required.

## How to apply

Use the form below to start an application. The board reviews every application within four weeks. If approved, we match the applicant with a sponsor at the start of the next term.
