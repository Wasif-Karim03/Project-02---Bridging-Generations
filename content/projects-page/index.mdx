## Applying for a project-funded scholarship

1. The applicant or their guardian fills out the scholarship application form.
2. The board reviews against the published eligibility criteria.
3. If approved, the student is matched with a project (school sponsorship, registration fee, materials drive, etc.).
4. A sponsor is matched. The student joins the program at the start of the next term.

## What you must keep doing

- Submit term grades within two weeks of the term ending.
- Tell us within seven days if you change school, address, or guardian.
- Treat mentors, teachers, and classmates with respect.

## What can end a scholarship

- Academic dishonesty.
- Repeated unexplained absence.
- Harassment of peers or staff.
- The family withdraws consent.

Most ended scholarships are paused first. Permanent revocation requires a board vote.
