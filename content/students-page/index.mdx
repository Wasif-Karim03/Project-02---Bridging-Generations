## Eligibility

- A current student at one of our partner schools in the Chittagong Hill Tracts.
- Family income falls below the regional threshold the board reviews each cycle.
- A signed letter of consent from a parent or legal guardian.

## What sponsorship covers

- Tuition for the term.
- Notebooks, textbooks, uniform, and exam fees.
- Daily lunch where the school provides a meal program.

## What we expect

- Maintain at least a passing GPA each term, reported by the school.
- Attend at least 85% of school days unless illness or family hardship is documented.
- Treat sponsors, mentors, teachers, and classmates with respect.
- No academic dishonesty.

## When sponsorship can be revoked

- Repeated failure to attend without documented cause.
- Academic dishonesty confirmed by the school.
- Repeated harassment or violence directed at peers or staff.
- The family withdraws consent.

Revocation is rare. Where possible, the board pauses sponsorship for one term and works with the family before ending it.
